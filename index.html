About Me

Ever since I was a child, I always had a love for technology. This interest provided me with the drive to independently research how it all worked. I'd spend hours on end learning bits and pieces of CSS, Java, and Ruby in order to get whatever little task I was working on up and running.

This drive sticks with me even today. If I don't know something, I look for a solution, learn how to implement it, and move on from there. It's a process that becomes quicker each time because once I've got an understanding of something, I can use that knowledge to solve any similar problems in the future.

I've been told by my peers that my teamwork skills are impressive. Whether it's taking charge and dividing up tasks among group members, brainstorming for ideas, or simply helping to reduce tension and prevent conflicts, I'm always ready and willing to assist my team in any way I can.
